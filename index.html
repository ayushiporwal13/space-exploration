Learn About New Telescopes!

James Webb Space Telescope (JWST)

Launch Date: Originally scheduled for December 2021 (might have changed).
Description: JWST is one of the most highly anticipated telescopes. It is a large, space-based observatory designed to provide unprecedented capabilities for observing the universe in infrared wavelengths. It is a collaboration between NASA, the European Space Agency (ESA), and the Canadian Space Agency (CSA).

Giant Magellan Telescope (GMT)

Expected Completion: Early 2020s (timeline may have changed).
Location: Las Campanas Observatory in Chile.
Description: GMT is an extremely large ground-based telescope currently under construction. It will have a primary mirror consisting of seven individual mirrors, each 8.4 meters in diameter, providing a total collecting area of 25.4 meters.
